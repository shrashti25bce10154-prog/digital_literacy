SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’T’S
Do's: 1. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all accounts. 
2. Use a professional profile picture. 
3. Verify information before sharing it.
 4. Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts. 
5. Network with industry professionals on platforms like LinkedIn.
Don'ts: 
1. Do not share your passwords or OTPs with anyone. 
2. Do not post sensitive personal details like your home address or ID numbers. 
3. Do not engage with internet trolls or cyberbullies. 
4. Do not click on suspicious links from unknown senders. 
5. Do not post content that could be seen as unprofessional by future employers.
